NLP Natural Language Processing
NLP is a systematic technique to processing human language. Language Processing is 
an area of Artificial Intelligence (AI) that uses computational method to understand
and generate human language NLP refers to set of algorithms,tasks and issues that 
take human created text as input.

And provide valuable information such as labels, semantic representations, and so on
as output. Other operations, such as translation,summarization,text generation as an 
output.

(Text Processing)
Text processing is a step in Natural Language Processing (NLP) that involves cleaning and transforming 
unstructured text data to prepare it for analysis , it is the first step in the NLP pipeline and has 
potential impact on the final process 

some common text proceeing tools include
tokenization
stemming
lemmitization
bag of words
